Alameda County Safe Parking Pilot Program

PROGRAM RULES & GUIDELINES

  1. During the Safe Parking Pilot program, we are able to offer stays of up to 90 days or until the end of the pilot, whichever comes first. Extensions will be considered in coordination with program staff and other service providers on a case-by-case basis.
  2. You are expected to ensure that your vehicle stays operational the entire time you are staying in the program. This is your responsibility. If you need help finding or paying for a mechanic, you must notify the program staff immediately.
  3. Your vehicle must stay inside your designated space. You are only allowed to have one vehicle in this program. All belongings must be kept inside your vehicle. Camping tarps or camping equipment beyond the top of the vehicle is prohibited.
  4. No fires or campstoves are allowed, inside or outside vehicles.
  5. Electrical hookup is not available. No extension cords may be run to/from your vehicle
  6. Quiet hours are from 10pm to 6am. At all other times, please keep the volume of conversations and any radio, music, or video devices at a low level so as to not disturb any of your neighbors in this program, in nearby programs, or in the residential area. Amplified sound heard 50 feet away from your vehicle is too loud.
  7. Littering in, around, or near the lot will not be tolerated. Dispose of all waste in designated areas for garbage, recycling, and compost.
  8. Park only in designated parking spots. Do not park in specially marked spaces ( e.g. disabled ).
  9. Guests are not permitted to stay with you or visit you at the site. You may not invite other people or visitors to the lot. Only accepted participants are allowed within the lot, and guests are not permitted for any length of time.
  10. It is your responsibility to comply with all applicable California state laws with regard to driving your vehicle. Alameda County and contracted service providers will not be liable for any damages to your vehicle or personal belongings
  11. If your vehicle is left unattended for more than 24 hours, we will attempt to contact you. If we are unable to contact you, and your vehicle remains continuously unattended for 72 hours, your vehicle may be towed away at your expense. If you anticipate being away from your vehicle for longer than one night, you must obtain advance permission.
  12. There are children, neighbors, and visitors in the immediate vicinity. Urinating or defecating outside of designated bathrooms or port-a-potties is strictly prohibited. Failure to abide by this rule will result in immediate termination from the program.
  13. The use, possession, or selling of illegal substances is strictly prohibited on site. Problematic behavior related to alcohol or drug use may result in you being exited from the property and/or terminated from the program.
  14. Firearms or weapons of any kind are strictly prohibited. Brandishing a gun or weapon will result in immediate termination.
  15. No violence, intimidation, verbal or physical abuse will be tolerated. No degrading, ethnic, racist, sexist, homophobic, transphobic, or other demeaning comments will be tolerated. Failure to abide by this rule may result in suspension or termination from the program.
  16. You are expected to obey all local, state and federal laws. Criminal conduct is strictly prohibited.
  17. Please notify us if you are will be leaving for more than 72 hours. If your spot remains vacant for 72 hours and we have not been notified of your return, you may be exited from the program so someone else can take the spot.
  18. Pets: You are expected to clean up pet waste immediately and dispose of it in the appropriate receptacle.
  19. Pets: Your pet must have a certificate of proof of the rabies vaccine
  20. Pets: Your pet must not be left unattended in your vehicle
  21. Pets: Your pet must not be tethered to any stationary object for more that 3 hours
  22. Pets: You pet must be on a leash at all times while outside of your vehicle

Failure to comply with these rules and regulations may result in termination from the Safe Parking Program. We reserve the right to terminate your participation in the program at any time, for any reason, without warning. Please review the Shelter Grievance Policy in this packet to understand your rights and the process for making a grievance. All complaints and program feedback will be taken seriously, and program staff will make every reasonable attempt to interview all parties involved.

Breaking some of these rules will result in immediate termination, as listed above. For all other rules, non-compliance will result in a verbal and/or written warning the first and second times. There will not be a third warning for non-compliance with the rules. At that point, your permit will be revoked.

I / We, accept and agree to respect, acknowledge and adhere to the rules and guidelines that are stated above and will accept full responsibility for the consequences that result of a violation of this contract.
